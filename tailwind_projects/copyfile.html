Empowering Technology to the community


Welcome to Camara Kenya's official website, where we showcase our commitment to using digital technology to empower students in education across Kenya. As an independent social enterprise, we believe that access to education is a fundamental right and a powerful tool for social and economic development.


Our work in Kenya includes teacher trainings and sensitization, infrastructure development, and community outreach. We work closely with schools, teachers, and community leaders to provide access to digital technology and training, as well as support in integrating it into the education system.

At Camara Kenya, we are proud to be at the forefront of the digital revolution in education. Our programs and initiatives have already impacted thousands of students across Kenya, and we continue to expand our reach every day.


We rely heavily on donor funding to achieve our goals, and we welcome your support. By donating to Camara Kenya, you can help us continue to provide access to education and digital technology to even more students and communities across Kenya.


Thank you for visiting our website and for your interest in Camara Kenya. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of students and communities across Kenya through the power of education.
